Our Certificate & Online Programs For 2024

University offers a wide range of graduate courses from postgraduate certificates to doctorates. Our courses are challenging and intensive and our approach to graduate study emphasises your ability to work independently, while supported by a world-class academic community.


Mobile Application Development Lab is a part of IV B. Tech I semester for IT students. The objective of this lab is to prepare students understanding of the fundamentals of Android operating systems, Install and configure Android application development tools Like Android Studio and Eclipse IDE.Students learn about intents, content providers,Emulators,layouts and SQLite etc. Student explores their practical knowledge on Applications in android environment, user interface applications, URL related applications using Java programming.


Computer Networks Lab/Web Programming Lab is a part of III B.Tech I semester for IT students. This lab provides information about working principle of various communication protocols, the network simulator environment and visualize a network topology and observe its performance, to analyze the traffic flow and the contents of protocol frames.


Software Engineering lab is a part of II B. Tech II semester Autonomous R20 Regulation for IT students. The purpose of the Software Engineering is to enable students to understand the Problem Analysis and Requirement Analysis. The Students can understand the project plan. The plan includes work assessments of the project, the schedule, available resources, and risk management. It helps us to create and specify software design based on the requirement specification through which prototype model can be built. We teach the student to learn how to develop a project plan, how to assess the risk, how to analyse risk information sheet etc. Also the students can come to learn about the various testing techniques with recommended systems/software requirements following the university prescribed textbooks and Lab manuals
